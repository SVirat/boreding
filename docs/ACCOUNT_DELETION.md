# Delete Your Account & Data

**Boreding — In-Flight Learning App**

> Boreding does not create server-side accounts. All your data is stored locally on your device. You have full control over deleting it at any time by following the steps below.

## Steps to Delete Your Data

### 1. Uninstall the Boreding app

Open your device **Settings → Apps → Boreding → Uninstall**. This permanently removes *all* locally stored data, including generated content, API keys, downloaded AI models, reading progress, payment status, and preferences.

### 2. Revoke Google access (if you connected Gmail)

Visit [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions), find **Boreding**, and click **Remove Access**. This revokes the app's read-only Gmail permission and invalidates any stored authentication tokens.

### 3. Request deletion of anonymous analytics data

Send an email to [svirat@gmail.com](mailto:svirat@gmail.com?subject=Delete%20my%20analytics%20data) with the subject line *"Delete my analytics data"*. We will remove any anonymous analytics events associated with your device within 30 days.

## What Data Is Deleted vs. Retained

| Data Type | Action | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Generated content & quizzes | ✅ Deleted | Removed immediately when you uninstall the app. |
| API keys (BYOK) | ✅ Deleted | Stored in on-device secure storage; removed on uninstall. |
| Offline AI models | ✅ Deleted | Model files in app storage are removed on uninstall. You can also delete them from within the app at any time. |
| Reading progress & preferences | ✅ Deleted | Removed immediately when you uninstall the app. |
| Payment status | ✅ Deleted | Confirmation ID stored locally; removed on uninstall. Razorpay retains its own transaction records per their [privacy policy](https://razorpay.com/privacy). |
| Google authentication tokens | ✅ Deleted | Removed on uninstall. Revoke access separately via [Google permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions). |
| Anonymous analytics events | ⏳ Retained up to 30 days | Anonymous, non-personally-identifiable usage events sent to Google Analytics. Deleted within 30 days upon email request. |

## Additional Information

- Boreding does **not** operate backend servers. There is no server-side account to delete.
- No personal data, emails, or content is stored outside your device.
- Anonymous analytics data cannot be linked back to your identity.
- After uninstalling and completing the steps above, no recoverable data remains.
